Our product

A focused portfolio, built around one exceptional product we believe in.

Soft drink · Brazil

Guaraná Antarctica

Guaraná Antarctica is one of Brazil's most beloved soft drinks — a refreshing, lightly sweet beverage made from the guaraná fruit native to the Amazon. Crisp, distinctive and instantly recognisable, it has been a favourite for well over a century.

  • Produced by Sumol+Compal, Portugal — authentic, European-made product.
  • A unique flavour that stands apart from cola and citrus sodas.
Sourcing

Sourced directly from Sumol+Compal, Portugal

We import Guaraná Antarctica straight from Sumol+Compal, one of Portugal's leading beverage producers and the official producer of Guaraná Antarctica for the European market.
